PRIVACY NOTICE

Privacy Notice for Open House Event

1. Introduction

Bangkok University is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information in connection with your participation in the Open House event.

2. Data We Collect

When you register for and attend the Open House event, we may collect the following personal information:

• Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address.

• Demographic Information: Age, gender, education level.

• Event-Related Information: Your interactions during the event, attendance records, and feedback.

• Multimedia: Photos, videos, or recordings taken during the event.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

• To manage your registration and participation in the Open House event.

• To communicate with you about the event, including updates, reminders, or any changes.

• To improve future events based on your feedback and participation.

• To promote Bangkok University, using multimedia content where you may appear, unless you opt out.

• To comply with legal requirements or respond to lawful requests.

4. Sharing Your Data

Your personal information may be shared with:

• Internal Staff: To manage the event and respond to your queries.

• Third-Party Service Providers: For event-related services, such as registration platforms, marketing tools, or security services, under strict confidentiality agreements.

• Authorities: If required by law or to protect our legal rights.

We will not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

Bangkok University takes appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights concerning your personal information:

• Access: You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

• Correction: You can request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.

• Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data, subject to legal or contractual limitations.

• Opt-out: You can opt-out of receiving promotional materials or being photographed at the event by informing us in advance.

7. Retention of Data

We will retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Notice, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

8. Cookies and Tracking

If you register through our website, we may use cookies or similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. For more details

9. Changes to This Privacy Notice

Bangkok University reserves the right to update this Privacy Notice at any time. Changes will be communicated to you through our website or directly via email.
